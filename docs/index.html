Privacy Policy for Tally

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tally mobile application ("Tally", "the app", "we", "us") handles information. Tally is an offline point-of-sale companion app (package id com.tallypos.app).

Summary: Tally does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All of your data stays on your device. We do not operate any server, and we have no access to anything you enter into the app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Tally has no analytics, no advertising, no crash-reporting, and no developer-operated backend. We never receive your information.

The following data is created by you and stored only on your device, in a local database that is private to the app:

  • Account credentials — a username and a PIN you create on first use. The PIN is never stored in clear text; it is stored as a salted SHA-256 hash.
  • Catalog items — item id/SKU, description, price, tax rate, and QR code value.
  • Sales records — carts and completed sales, including line items and totals.

This information never leaves your device. We cannot see it, retrieve it, or restore it.

Camera Permission

Tally requests permission to use your device's camera for a single purpose: to scan QR codes and barcodes so you can ring up items.

  • Scanning is performed entirely on your device in real time.
  • The app does not take or save photos, and does not record or transmit any video, images, or camera data.
  • Camera access is used only while you are on the scanning screen. You may type codes manually instead, and you can revoke camera permission at any time in your device settings.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Tally offers an optional subscription (with a free trial) processed through Google Play Billing. When you start a trial or subscribe:

  • The transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not see, collect, or store your payment details (such as card numbers).
  • The app only checks with Google Play whether your subscription is active in order to unlock paid functionality.
  • Your purchase is governed by Google's terms and the Google Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The only third party involved is Google Play, and only if you choose to subscribe. See the Google Privacy Policy. Tally does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone, because we never have access to it in the first place.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because all of your data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of it. You can delete individual records inside the app, or remove all app data by uninstalling Tally or clearing the app's storage in your device settings. Once removed, the data cannot be recovered.

Children's Privacy

Tally is a business/productivity tool intended for adult users and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Security

Your data is stored in the app's private, sandboxed storage area provided by your operating system. PINs are stored only as salted SHA-256 hashes, never in clear text.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and posted at the same URL.
